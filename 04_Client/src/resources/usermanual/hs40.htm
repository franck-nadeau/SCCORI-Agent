Using the SCCORI Agents

Once you have started the SCCORI Agents:

  • You must enter your own information into the SCCORI simulation site.
  • Select the same meeting room.
  • Play your position. The SCCORI Agents are quite responsive, so the your next turn won't take too long. Don't go away!
  • The simulation will block if you don't play your turn. This may eventually cause your agents to time out, or even encourage another user to RIP your unused agents. Please play promptly.
